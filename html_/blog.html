How to make naan

This blog is about how to make butter naan in an easy and simple way

Naan is a soft, fluffy, and versatile bread that pairs perfectly with a wide variety of dishes, from curries to grilled meats and vegetables.

Its light texture and slightly chewy bite make it satisfying to eat, while its ability to be customized with garlic, herbs, or seeds adds extra flavor and nutrition.

Naan is quick to cook, easy to serve, and can be enjoyed by itself or used to scoop up sauces.
