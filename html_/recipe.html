This page is clearly tells about how to make naan

If you want a perfect naan just follow the upcoming steps

lets start
  1. Use whole wheat flour (atta) instead of all-purpose flour – adds fiber.
  2. Add yogurt or milk – gives protein and makes it soft naturally.
  3. Skip or reduce butter/ghee – brush lightly or use olive oil.
  4. Add seeds – flax, sesame, or nigella seeds boost nutrients.
  5. Stuff wisely – avoid heavy cheese; use veggies or paneer sparingly.
