Tips for Making Perfect Naan

Follow these simple tips to make your naan soft, fluffy, and delicious!

Important Tips:

  • Use warm water or milk to activate the yeast properly.
  • Knead the dough well for at least 8–10 minutes for a soft texture.
  • Let the dough rest and rise in a warm place for at least 1 hour.
  • Preheat the pan or oven to high heat before cooking naan.
  • Brush lightly with butter or ghee after cooking for extra flavor.
  • For extra taste, sprinkle garlic, herbs, or seeds on top before baking.
  • Do not overload with cheese or toppings — it can make naan dense.
